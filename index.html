About Me

I am an Associate Developer at D.E.Shaw, working in the LiveDesign team under Schrodinger which provides a platform for material science and drug discovery.

Before joining D.E. Shaw, I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Ropar in Computer Science and Engineering. After my third year, I had the opportunity to intern in the Data Science team at LinkBal, Tokyo.

A full-stack developer at D.E. Shaw, where I am the engineering owner of LiveDesign Admin and LiveDesign Learning projects. Also, responsible for various features on the main application. We work on a variety of open-source languages such as Java, Python, Django, React JS, Docker.

Linkbal, Tokyo (Japan)

Data Science Intern

Summer intern in LinkBal, where I worked on a project for predicting the cancellation probability of an event based on the available data for the past events posted by them on their portal. Customers are more likely to buy events at the top of the application, hence, sorting this order to show events more likely to be successful at the top improves customer base and company reputation, which we aimed to predict accurately during this intern. Through a combination of deep neural networks and catboost decision tree classifiers and in-depth anaysis of the features, we were able to achieve high accuracy for this prediction.
